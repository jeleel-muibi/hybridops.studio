# Contributing (Portfolio Policy)

**HybridOps.Studio** is a public, evidence-backed portfolio. To keep the project stable, reviewable, and aligned with its documentation, **external pull requests are not accepted**. You are welcome to open **issues** for clarifications, documentation fixes, or questions.

- **Reuse:** Roles and modules may be published separately (for example Ansible Galaxy or Terraform Registry) or scaffolded under `contrib/`. See `contrib/README.md` for how to publish your own copies.
- **Forks:** Code is licensed under MIT-0. You are free to fork and adapt it for your environment; attribution is appreciated.
- **Security:** Please follow `SECURITY.md` for reporting vulnerabilities.
- **Pull requests:** Closed by policy so the portfolio remains read-only and reproducible.
- **Engagements:** See [`CONTRACTING.md`](./CONTRACTING.md) for services and contact details.
